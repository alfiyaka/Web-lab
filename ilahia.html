ILAHIA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHBOLOGY

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), established in 2002 in Muvattupuzha, Kerala, is a self-financing institution affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE. Set on a serene 30-acre campus along the Muvattupuzhaâ€“Kothamangalam Road, the college offers a peaceful and modern environment for technical education. ICET provides undergraduate programs in various engineering fields including Civil, Mechanical, Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Robotics, along with postgraduate courses like M.Tech, MBA, MCA, and Ph.D. programs. With advanced infrastructure featuring smart classrooms, well-equipped labs, a large library, and over 700 computers, the college also offers separate hostels, sports facilities, and active student clubs such as NSS, IEEE, and IEDC. Known for its supportive campus life, regular technical and cultural events, and scholarship opportunities, ICET is committed to producing competent, ethical, and socially responsible professionals under its motto "Dynamism, Growth and Selfless Service."
